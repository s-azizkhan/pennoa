## 1. Name

**Pennoa** /ˈpɛn.oʊ.ə/ — *PEN-oh-ah*

### Etymology
From *pennon* (Old French *penon*) — the long, narrow banner trailing from a knight's lance or a ship's mast. Pennoa softens the heraldic root into a modern, ownable form ending in open vowels.

### Linguistic Construction
- **Plosive P** — energy, precision, reliability (BlackBerry-class consonant)
- **Double nasal NN** — warmth, nurture, approachability
- **Trailing -oa** — refined, calm, premium release
- **CVCVV rhythm** — exceptional processing fluency (Dasani-class structure)

### Why This Name
1. Most precise semantic match — *pennon* literally *is* the trailing banner the product depicts.
2. Distinctive — zero collision with notification/calendar category codes (`Cal-`, `Meet-`, `-ly`, `Remind-`).
3. Literary register matches refined, designer-first audience without slipping into cute or corporate.
4. Available `.com` + `.app` (verified) — rare for short, ownable names.

## 2. Positioning

### One-Liner
*Meeting reminders that respect your flow.*

### Elevator Pitch
Pennoa drifts a tiny airplane across your screen ten and five minutes before each meeting, trailing a banner you can't miss but won't resent. Built for makers who hate calendar pop-ups.

### Positioning Frame (Dunford)

| Element | Definition |
|---|---|
| **Competitive Alternative** | Native macOS / Google Calendar pop-up alerts — dismissed reflexively, lost in notification fatigue. |
| **Unique Attributes** | Floating cinematic airplane + banner above all apps. Non-blocking. Two-pulse cadence (T-10 / T-5). Animation *is* the signal. |
| **Value** | Never miss a meeting *and* keep flow state intact. Reminder becomes a moment of delight, not anxiety. |
| **Target Market** | Mac creatives & makers — designers, developers, writers — in deep work, allergic to corporate-style alerts. |
| **Market Category** | Ambient meeting reminders. |

### Point of View
Notifications became noise. Calendar alerts trained users to dismiss without reading. Flow deserves better than an X-button reflex. Reminders can be theatre — brief, charming, unmissable, then gone.

### Category Bet
We are not building a better notification. We are reframing the alert itself as the product. Adjacent to Cron / Amie / Fantastical, but distinct: those apps reorganize your calendar. Pennoa replaces the moment of interruption.

## 4. Voice & Tone

### Voice Attributes
- **Refined.** Designed, considered. Never goofy or quirky-startup.
- **Warm.** Human, friendly. Never sterile or corporate.
- **Quiet-confident.** Speaks softly because it doesn't need to shout.

### What the Voice IS / IS NOT

| IS | IS NOT |
|---|---|
| Confident | Arrogant |
| Warm | Cutesy |
| Designed | Decorated |
| Witty | Sarcastic |
| Direct | Curt |

### Copy Rules
- No exclamation marks (except in product easter eggs).
- No "Hey there!" greetings.
- No emoji in product UI. Sparingly in marketing if at all.
- Verbs over adjectives. Show motion.
- Lowercase headlines when it serves the calm register.
- Never use the word "delightful" — *show* delight; don't claim it.

### Forbidden Words
*delightful, magical, seamless, frictionless, revolutionary, game-changer, simply, just, basically, leverage, unlock, supercharge*

## 6. Visual Identity Direction

### Logomark
A horizontal wordmark whose motion *is* its identity. Static on dark surfaces; on the product surface it drifts left-to-right slowly, trailing a hairline banner. The mark behaves like the product. (Pentagram "kinetic identity" — time as material.)

### Typography
- **Display:** GT Sectra, Tiempos Headline, or Söhne Breit — a refined serif or condensed sans with character. Carries the literary register of the name.
- **Body:** Inter, Söhne, or Geist — neutral, readable, modern.
- **Mono (product surfaces):** Berkeley Mono or JetBrains Mono.

Avoid: rounded display sans (Circular, Poppins), generic geometric (Montserrat).

### Color
- **Primary:** Warm signal hue — golden-hour amber `#E8A75C` or vintage-poster ochre.
- **Ink:** Deep aviation navy `#0E1A2B` (not pure black).
- **Paper:** Off-white parchment `#F4EFE6`.
- **Accent:** Single muted contrast — faded poppy or sea-green for state changes.

Map against competitors: Cron/Amie/Fantastical lean cool blue + white. Pennoa owns warm + paper.

### Motion Principles
- **Drift, never pop.** Easing: long entrance, slow middle, gentle exit.
- **The banner is the message.** Never decorate with extra UI.
- **Five-second life.** Reminder enters, conveys, leaves. No persistent state.
- **Respect the cursor.** Never block click-through.

### Illustration
Hand-drawn parchment-banner illustrations. Mid-century travel poster influence (Saul Bass, David Klein, vintage Pan Am). Never stock. Never 3D-rendered isometric SaaS art.

## 9. Category POV (long-form)

**The observation.** Software notifications have collapsed into a single undifferentiated stream. Calendar alerts, Slack pings, email pops, system updates — all rendered in the same gray pill, all dismissed with the same reflex.

**The gap.** A meeting reminder isn't the same as a software update, but the operating system treats them identically. The cost of a missed meeting is high. The cost of dismissing one more notification is zero. So we dismiss.

**The ramification.** Knowledge workers either miss meetings (status quo) or live inside their calendar app to compensate (anxiety). Neither preserves flow.

**The vision.** A meeting reminder should be a visual event, not a notification payload. Brief enough not to break flow. Distinctive enough not to be dismissed. Designed enough to be welcomed.

**The solution.** Pennoa — ambient meeting reminders, rendered as cinematic moments above your work.
